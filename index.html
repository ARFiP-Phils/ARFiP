About Us

The Alliance of Registered Fisheries Professionals Inc. (ARFiP) is a non-stock, non-profit organization dedicated to uniting and advancing the fisheries profession in the Philippines. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism and ethics, fostering collaboration, and advocating for the sustainable management of our aquatic resources.

Our Purpose & Mission

Promote Professionalism

To promote the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct among fisheries professionals in the Philippines.

Advance Knowledge

To advance the knowledge, science, and technology in fisheries through research, education, training, and extension.

Advocate for Policies

To advocate for policies and practices that promote sustainable fisheries management and conservation of aquatic resources.

Foster Collaboration

To foster collaboration and networking among fisheries professionals, government agencies, academic institutions, and other stakeholders.

Protect Rights & Welfare

To protect the rights and welfare of fisheries professionals and advocate for their professional development and recognition.

Source of Information

To serve as a credible source of information and expertise on fisheries.

Promote Public Awareness

To promote public awareness and understanding of the importance of fisheries and aquatic resources for sustainable development.

Uphold Inclusivity

To uphold the principles of inclusivity, diversity, and gender equality in all aspects of fisheries management and professional practice.
